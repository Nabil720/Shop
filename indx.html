The phone also features a new titanium design, a redesigned camera control, and is touted for its long battery life and improved performance.

IPhone 15 PRO

The iPhone 15 Pro is a high-end smartphone featuring a titanium design, an advanced A17 Bionic chip,and a pro-level camera system. It boasts a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with Dynamic Island and Always-On display,and is available in multiple storage options up to 1TB. The camera system includes a 48MP main sensor and 12MP ultra-wide and telephoto lenses.
